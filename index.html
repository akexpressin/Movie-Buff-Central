About Star Wars

Star Wars is one of the most iconic movie franchises of all time, created by George Lucas. With its epic storylines, memorable characters, and groundbreaking visual effects, Star Wars has influenced generations of movie-goers and pop culture.

The Rebel Alliance launches an all-out assault on the Empire, while Luke Skywalker confronts Darth Vader and Emperor Palpatine for the fate of the galaxy.
